About

Have you ever tried to make a playlist on a music streaming website just to find that the site didn't have a certain song you wanted to add?

Playall.audio solves this problem by allowing you to create a playlist using songs from multiple streaming sources.

How To:

  1. Type the name of the song you wish to add to your playlist in the 'Search Bar'.
  2. Click the logo of a music streaming site to search for results.
  3. A list of search results should display below the 'Search Bar'.
    Click a search result to add it to your playlist.
  4. You can add multiple songs from any supported streaming site together in one playlist.
    The 'Playlist Bar' on the right side of the screen allows you to choose another song in your playlist to play, remove a song, skip forwards / backwards, and play / pause.

Supported Sources: SoundCloud, YouTube
